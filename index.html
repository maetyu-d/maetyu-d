About Me

I am a researcher and educator specialising
in the convergence of arts and technology,
with a focus on the intersection of music,
Human-Computer Interaction, and video games.
Alongside my longstanding interest in
experimental electronic music, my teaching
and research encompass areas such as sound
for games and film (technical, creative,
and theoretical aspects), musical interface
and controller design, sound and video
synthesis (especially modular synthesizers),
and the design of assistive technologies.
I am currently a Senior Lecturer in Game
Audio and Technical Design at Staffordshire
University, a Leonardo AI Lab fellow, a
trustee of The OHMI Trust, Accessibility
Chair for NIME 2026, a reviewer for various
sound and interaction-related conferences
and journals, and EE for MSc Music
Engineering and Production at the
University of South Wales.
